CAD Drafting Technician

Assisting the Project Managers this individual must be a motivated self-starter. Able to read architectural drawings. Prepare fabrication drawings of pre-glazed curtain wall systems and architectural fenestration products for institutional, commercial and industrial construction. Projects ranging from small storefronts to large multi-story office buildings. Drafting (Glazing) to support our manufacturing process. Must be proficient in Word, Excel and AutoCad.

Skills/Education

Minimum 4 years direct industry experience in curtain wall design and Construction required • Must be familiar with unitized curtain wall design and installation • Modeling / Inventor software experience an asset • Strong verbal and written communication skills • Must be organized and self-motivated • Solid construction knowledge base with emphasis on Building Envelope Design • Strong PC skills in AutoCAD ,MS Office • Strong time management skills to manage multiple projects in a fast paced contemporary environment • Ability to work independently and in a team environment • Strong mathematical skills are an asset

Duties

Provide complete fabrication drawings and bills of materials for each job Design and draft detailed drawings for architectural approval, shop fabrication & field installations Reviewing shop releases to check for accuracy Ordering materials from suppliers Create custom designs as required for architectural approval
