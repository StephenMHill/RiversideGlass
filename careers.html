Architectural Drafter

Qualifications

  • • Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, or a Diploma in Architectural Technology or Construction Management
  • • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • • Must be organized and self-motivated
  • • Solid construction knowledge base
  • • Strong PC skills in AutoCAD, and MS Office
  • • Strong time management skills to manage multiple projects in a fast paced contemporary environment
  • • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
  • • Strong mathematical skills are an asset

Duties

  • • Analyze technical documents and reports
  • • Produce quality designs, construction drawings and details
  • • Conduct research related to projects
  • • Maintain consistant flow of information to ensure discrepencies are resolved efficently

Project Manager

Qualifications

  • • Engineering degree or diploma, or equivalent trade experience
  • • Sound written and verbal communication skills
  • • Excellent management skills
  • • Well organized, proven ability to work under tight time constraints
  • • Ability to travel if required
  • • Windows based computer skills required
  • • Strong negotiation skills
  • • Train, grow, improve, and perfect your craft
  • • Track key measures, Engineering Change Requests, Engineering Change Notices, customer Purchase Orders
  • • Monitor project timelines, sample submissions, customer buyoff

    • Duties

  • • Coordinate and supervise employee teams and subcontractors
  • • Review all aspects of available contracts as well as orders, estimates, pre-fab information and operations, for any deficiencies that would delay or prevent the signing of documentation
  • • Manage and coordinate the purchasing, tracking, fabricating, handling, and shipment of raw and finished goods amongst various offices and job sites
  • • Establish and continually update job schedules, site requirements, as well as regularly consulting general contractor and supervisors
  • • Visit client sites to take dimensions and determine project needs
  • • Communicate with other management and vendors to troubleshoot any discrepancies such as deadline delays, architectural design changes, scheduling conflicts and the logistics of mishandled or lost materials
  • • Assist in the planning and scheduling of current and upcoming projects
  • • Developed and improved processes and procedures to increase employee efficiency, team cohesion and finished product quality

    • Fabricator

    Qualifications

  • • Minimum high school diploma required
  • • Proficient in reading a tape measure
  • • Effectively and safely operate hand tools, power tools, and other various types of machinery such as saw, drill press, and router.
  • • Must be able to do repetitive motions (drilling, cleaning, and application of fasteners)
  • • Ability to lift up to 75 lbs. on a regular basis
  • • Must be able to commit to required working hours including potential overtime
  • • General interpersonal skills.
  • • Good team working skills
  • • Previous fabrication experience preferred

  • • Perform standard shop maintenance and daily expectations as directed by the supervisor.
  • • Assist in the loading, unloading, and shipment of materials and finished goods to and from vendors and job sites
  • • Ensure that fabrication is accurate to the outlined specifications and maintain quality standards
  • • Assemble and fit metal components as directed by supervisor or by reading detailed drawings.
  • • Become familair with all aspects of the fabrication process
  • • Dependable attendence is a must
